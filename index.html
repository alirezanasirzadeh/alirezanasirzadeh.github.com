About

Hello, my name is Ali and I'm ... Well, I'm still figuring it out who I am, but I can break people down into forces, moments, and muscle activations ... professionally, of course.

I'm deeply passionate about working at the intersection of sports and movement biomechanics and wearable robotics. My research focuses on designing and biomechanically validating exosuits and assistive devices that actively improve human movement, dynamic balance, and athletic performance. To fully understand how the human body moves, I combine advanced biomechanical measurements and detailed musculoskeletal modeling to evaluate gait, dynamic stability, and joint loading. Ultimately, my goal is to translate these complex biomechanical insights into intuitive, human-centered wearable technology.
